My Message

Patience, single-minded desire, meticulous skill along with our intelligence and time, we are different from each other. We achieve so many honors and titles. The proper use of time does not end only at the end of student life, but from there we learn punctuality and discipline and start being punctual and disciplined. As a result, people acquire individual qualities.

Writing a book is a very honorable job. You can get even more honor if you know how to read books. Keeping this idea in mind, I am always alert and tirelessly running day and night on the path of acquiring knowledge. There was a time when I thought that I would also write books. Without talent, a person can never earn people's love. Due to thinking about poetry and practicing it more, I started mixing my feet from a very young age. Whenever a person tries to develop his talent or wants to do something in life with it, he starts getting blocked in various ways. I am not above it either. By nurturing self-confidence, I have become a young poet today. Alhamdulillah. In the future, I will be able to contribute a lot to the Bengali language and literature. Insha Allah. I am moving forward on the path of my dreams by accepting everything.

My Writing Journey

Since childhood, he felt a pull towards poetry and feeling love for his mother tongue, Bengali, Charan started writing slowly from the age of sixteen. He wrote his first poem titled 'Manab Jiban'. Which made him known as a poet to his friends and teachers.

With his Sunipur writing and touch of technology, he wants to reach the Bengali language and literature to the hearts of the mother tongue lovers and country lovers.
